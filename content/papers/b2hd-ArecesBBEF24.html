Leveraging Language Models and Automatic Summarization in Online Programming Learning Environments

Abstract

This article describes the results of a collaborative project by researchers from the Universidad Nacional de C&oacute;rdoba in Argentina and IKUMI SRL, an ed-tech company that provides massive-scale solutions for teaching programming. IKUMI develops educational resources in virtual spaces, including an open source platform called Mumuki,a which offers didactic paths with automated assessment of programming exercises.
