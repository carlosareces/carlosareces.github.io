Abstract

We provide a detailed analysis of very weak fragments of modal logics. Our fragments lack connectives that introduce non-determinism and they feature restrictions on the modal operators, which may lead to substantial reductions in complexity. Our main result is a general game-based characterization of the expressive power of our fragments over the class of finite structures.
