Characterization Results for d-Horn Formulas

C. Areces, V. Becher, and S. Ferro. Characterization Results for d-Horn Formulas. In Proceedings of ITALLC'98. Information Theoretic Approaches to Logic, Language and Computation, Hsi-Tow, Taiwan, 1998.

Abstract

We provide two di erent model theoretic characterizations of a new fragment of first-order logic which we call d-Horn formulas. This fragment is dual to the well know Horn fragment and have the same complexity for provability. The method used in the characterization (syntactic translation functions between formulas which are mimicked by translation functions between models) might be applied to characterize other first-order restrictions.
