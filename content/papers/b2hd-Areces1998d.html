Abstract

We consider two recently proposed defini- tions of the expressive power of description logics, one due to Baader, the other due to Kurtonina and de Rijke. The proposals are non-equivalent, and we explain their differences, similarities, as well as ways in which they are related. We also map out a research agenda resulting from efforts to combine the two approaches.
