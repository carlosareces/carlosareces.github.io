A Bit about Myself

1. Or 0, you choose. I suppose both are equally true about me.

But in addition to that, I can tell you that I was born in Argentina, more specifically in Buenos Aires (down south in the map, at the end of South America) years ago.

Currently, I'm an Full Professor at the LIIS group at Computer Science Section, of the Facultad de Matemática, Astronomía y Física, Universidad Nacional de Córdoba. Since December 2010, I am also an Independent Researcher of the Consejo Nacional de Investigaciones Científicas y Técnicas (CONICET).

Till 2010, I was an INRIA permanent Researcher (CR1) at the Talaris team at LORIA. Before that, up to 2002 I was a post-doc at the STeFI Project, in Amsterdam, where I finished my studies on October 12th, 2000, obtaining my PhD degree at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation at the University of Amsterdam, The Netherlands.

Before that, I studied for one year at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Warwick, England.

And even before, I studied six years at my home unversity Universidad de Buenos Aires at the Department of Computer Science, where I obtained my degree of Licenciado en Ciencias de la Computacion.
