INFINIS

INFINIS was a French-Argentinean Laboratory (Laboratoire Internationale Associé) between Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS) and Université Paris Diderot, on the one hand, and Consejo Nacional de Investigaciones Cientéficas y Técnicas (CONICET) and the Universidad de Buenos Aires, on the other. It is devoted to research in Computer Science. Specific focus is placed on formal methods, for modeling, verification and development of complex software artifacts.

Research

Our group specializes in computational logic, with emphasis in propositional and modal logics. We investigate themes in automated deduction, algorithmic complexity, combining theories, SMT solving, expressivity, formal verification, etc.
